Who We Are

Mustard Seed Harvest Ministries Inc.

We are a group of people united by faith in Jesus Christ and called to live out His gospel. Just as a tiny mustard seed grows into something far greater than its size suggests, we believe God takes ordinary people and uses them to bring an extraordinary harvest of faith, hope, and transformed lives.

We invite you to journey with us — whether you're searching for answers, looking for community, or ready to grow deeper in your walk with Christ.

Our Mission

Leading People To Christ And Building Disciples For God's Glory

Everything we do — from Sunday worship to mid-week fellowships and missional communities — flows from this single calling: to introduce people to Jesus and walk with them as they grow into mature, fruitful disciples.

Our Vision

To see lives transformed by the gospel and a community rooted in genuine faith, love, and service to God and to one another.

Our Values

Christ-centered worship, biblical teaching, authentic community, and a heart of service guide everything we do as a church.

Our Heart To Serve

We believe every believer is called to serve. We equip and send our members to bless our church, our families, and our city.

Get Involved

Find Your Place To Serve

From music and worship to missional communities, outreach, and hospitality — there's a ministry where your gifts can make a difference. We'd love to help you find where you belong.
