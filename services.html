Join Us In Worship

Come As You Are, Worship With Your Heart

Every gathering at Mustard Seed Harvest Ministries is an invitation to encounter God's presence through heartfelt worship, anointed music, and the proclamation of His Word. Whether you're new to faith or have walked with Christ for years, there's a place for you here.
